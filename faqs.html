Frequently Asked Questions

What will you sell?

An application that is used to match the characteristics of potential pet owners and prospective pets in local shelters and pet stores.

Who will buy it?

The survey suggests that about 70% of people interested in buying pets in the future would like to use this service. Of the people wanting to use this service, 54.7% of them would be willing to pay based on the rates and the rest said they would use it even if advertisements were present if they could use it for free.

How will your business idea help people?

This service provides people who are looking for a pet to be matched with animals that fit their characteristics and lifestyle. It will also benefit shelters by hopefully lowering the return rate on animals.

What will you charge?

I would like to charge $10 for every download. I feel as though that is a reasonable cost to help people be matched with an ideal pet for their personal life style and characteristics.
