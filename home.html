Successful Match

Brad and Bear

Brad wanted to adopt a pet but he couldn't decide on what kind. He was thinking about a large dog but couldn't decide if it was a right fit for his lifestyle since he lives in an apartment complex.

Brad is a prime candidate for the PetMate app. He answered various questions about his current lifestyle and living arrangements and the shelter filled out a lengthy form about the personality and needs of all their animals.

Looking at both what Brad can offer and what Bear needs a match can be made. It takes the guess work out of owning a pet and makes it better for both the pet and the owner.
